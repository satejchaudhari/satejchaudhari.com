About

Who's writing this

I'm a security researcher focused on vulnerability assessment, network security, and hands-on red teaming. Most of what ends up here started as notes I took while working through a target, a lab, or a CTF — cleaned up enough to be useful to someone else running into the same problem.

This site is deliberately simple: no build step, no tracking, just writeups, field notes, and a toolkit page I keep updated with what's actually in my kit.
